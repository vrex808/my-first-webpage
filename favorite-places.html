These are my favorite places to visit when back home in Hawaii

I was born in Honolulu, HI but have grown up in Redmond, WA pretty much my whole life. I would go back home each Summer and stay there all Summer long. The Diamond Head would have to be one of my favorite hikes on the island.

Waianae is on the beautiful Westside of the island

Westside of Oahu brings us to Waianae. I'd say it's more of a laid back, less busy destination where I like to enjoy my time. There are a few great local food spots and snack spots to get my favorit dish, Laulau, and my favorite treat, malosadas.
